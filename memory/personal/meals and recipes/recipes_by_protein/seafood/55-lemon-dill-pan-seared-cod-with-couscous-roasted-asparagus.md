# 🍣 Lemon Dill Pan-Seared Cod with Couscous & Roasted Asparagus

**Serves 6**

### Ingredients

- Cod:
- 4 cod fillets (about 1½ lbs total)
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1 lemon (juice + zest)
- 2 tbsp fresh dill, chopped (or 1 tbsp dried)
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- Couscous:
- 1½ cups dry couscous
- 1¾ cups chicken broth (or water)
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Pinch salt
- Roasted Asparagus:
- 1 lb asparagus, trimmed
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper
- Lemon wedges for serving

### Instructions

1. **Asparagus:** Preheat oven to 425°F. Toss asparagus with olive oil, salt, pepper on a sheet pan. Roast 12-15 min until tender-crisp and lightly charred at the tips.
2. **Couscous:** Bring chicken broth to a boil in a small saucepan. Stir in couscous, olive oil, salt. Cover, remove from heat. Rest 5 min. Fluff with fork.
3. **Cod:** Pat fillets dry, season with salt and pepper. Heat olive oil in large skillet over medium-high. Sear cod 3-4 min per side until golden and flakes easily with a fork.
4. **Pan sauce:** Reduce heat to medium. Add butter, lemon juice, lemon zest, garlic, dill to the pan. Spoon over cod for 1 min to meld flavors.
5. **Serve:** Plate couscous, top with cod, asparagus on the side. Garnish with lemon wedges and extra fresh dill.
