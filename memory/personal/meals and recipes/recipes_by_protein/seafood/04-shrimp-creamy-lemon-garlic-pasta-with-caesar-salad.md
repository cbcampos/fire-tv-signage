# 🍤 Shrimp Creamy Lemon Garlic Pasta with Caesar Salad

**Serves 6**

## 🍤 Pasta

### Ingredients

- 1½ lb shrimp
- 1 lb linguine or spaghetti
- 3–4 garlic cloves
- ¾ cup cream or milk
- ½ cup grated Parmesan
- Zest and juice of 1 lemon
- 2 tbsp butter or olive oil
- Salt and pepper
Cook pasta. Sauté garlic in butter/oil. Add shrimp until pink. Stir in cream, lemon, Parmesan. Toss with pasta.

## 🥗 Caesar Salad

- 2 heads romaine
- ¾ cup Caesar dressing
- ½ cup croutons
- ½ cup Parmesan
Toss and serve.
