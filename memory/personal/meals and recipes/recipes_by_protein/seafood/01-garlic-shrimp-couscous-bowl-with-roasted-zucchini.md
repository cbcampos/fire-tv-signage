# 🍤 Garlic Shrimp & Couscous Bowl with Roasted Zucchini

**Serves 6**

## 🍤 Garlic Shrimp

### Ingredients

- 2 lbs shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 4 garlic cloves, minced
- Salt and pepper

### Instructions

1. Heat oil in skillet.
2. Add garlic; cook 30 seconds.
3. Add shrimp and cook 3–4 minutes until opaque.

## 🍚 Couscous with Peas

### Ingredients

- 2 cups dry couscous
- 2 cups water or broth
- 1½ cups peas
Prepare couscous per package instructions. Stir in peas.

## 🥒 Roasted Zucchini

### Ingredients

- 4 zucchini, sliced
- Olive oil
- Garlic powder
Roast at 400°F for 20 minutes.

## 🍋 Lemon Tahini Drizzle

### Ingredients

- ⅓ cup tahini
- Juice of 1½ lemons
- 2 tbsp water
- 1 garlic clove
Whisk until smooth and drizzle over bowls.
