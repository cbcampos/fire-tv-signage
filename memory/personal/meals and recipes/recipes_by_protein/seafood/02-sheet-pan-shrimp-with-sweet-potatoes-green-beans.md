# 🍤 Sheet Pan Shrimp with Sweet Potatoes & Green Beans

**Serves 6**

### Ingredients

- 2¼ lb shrimp, peeled
- 4 sweet potatoes, cut into wedges
- 1½ lb green beans, trimmed
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 2 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- ½ tsp black pepper

## 🥣 Yogurt-Lemon Dip

- 1½ cups Greek yogurt
- 1 tbsp lemon juice

### Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
2. Toss sweet potatoes with oil and spices. Roast 15 minutes.
3. Add green beans and roast 10 minutes more.
4. Toss shrimp with oil and seasoning. Add to pan and roast 6–8 minutes.
5. Serve with yogurt dip.
