# Garlic Butter Shrimp Scampi over Linguine

**Serves 6 adults**

## Ingredients

- 1½ lb large shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 12 oz linguine
- 6 tbsp butter
- 5 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
- ½ cup dry white wine (or chicken broth)
- Juice of 2 lemons
- ¼ tsp chili flake
- ½ cup flat-leaf parsley, chopped
- Salt and pepper
- ½ cup grated parmesan for serving

### Simple Arugula Salad
- 6 cups arugula
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Pinch of salt
- Cracked pepper

## Instructions

1. Cook linguine in large pot salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve 1 cup pasta water.
2. While pasta cooks, melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add garlic and chili flake, cook 2–3 min until fragrant.
3. Add shrimp to skillet in single layer. Cook 2 min per side until pink. Add wine/lemon juice, simmer 2 min.
4. Add drained pasta and ¼ cup pasta water to skillet. Toss to coat. Add more pasta water if needed for sauciness.
5. Remove from heat, toss with parsley and parmesan.
6. Toss arugula with lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Serve salad alongside pasta.
