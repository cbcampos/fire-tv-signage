# 🍣 Baked Teriyaki Salmon with Roasted Broccoli & Quinoa

**Serves 6**

## 🍣 Salmon

### Ingredients

- 6 salmon fillets (5–6 oz each)
- ½ cup teriyaki sauce
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Optional: sesame seeds, green onions

### Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
2. Line baking sheet with parchment.
3. Brush salmon with teriyaki sauce and drizzle lightly with olive oil.
4. Bake 12–15 minutes until flaky.

## 🥦 Roasted Broccoli

### Ingredients

- 1 large head broccoli (or 32 oz florets)
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp teriyaki sauce
- Salt and pepper
Toss and roast at 400°F for 20 minutes.

## 🍚 Quinoa

### Ingredients

- 1½ cups dry quinoa
- 3 cups water or broth
- Salt to taste
Rinse quinoa. Simmer covered 15 minutes. Fluff and rest 5 minutes before serving.
