# Blackened Shrimp Tacos with Mango Salsa

**Serves 6 adults**

## Ingredients

### Shrimp
- 1½ lb large shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 2 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp onion powder
- ½ tsp cayenne
- 1 tsp thyme
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper

### Mango Salsa
- 2 mangoes, diced
- ½ red onion, finely diced
- 1 jalapeño, seeded and minced
- Juice of 2 limes
- Handful of fresh cilantro, chopped

### Tacos
- 12 small flour tortillas
- Shredded cabbage
- Creme fresca or sour cream
- Lime wedges

## Instructions

1. Mix shrimp with paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, cayenne, thyme, salt, and pepper.
2. Heat oil in a large skillet over high heat. Cook shrimp 2–3 min per side until blackened and opaque.
3. Toss mango, red onion, jalapeño, lime juice, and cilantro for salsa.
4. Warm tortillas. Assemble tacos with shrimp, mango salsa, cabbage, and creme fresca. Squeeze lime over top.
