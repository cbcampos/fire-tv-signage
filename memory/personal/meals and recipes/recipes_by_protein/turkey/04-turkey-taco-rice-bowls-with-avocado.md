# 🌮 Turkey Taco Rice Bowls with Avocado

**Serves 6**

### Ingredients

- 2¼ lb ground turkey
- 3 tbsp taco seasoning
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 red bell pepper, diced
- 1 cup frozen corn
- 3 cups cooked brown rice
- 3 ripe avocados
- Juice of 1 lime
- Salt to taste

### Instructions

1. Cook rice if not pre-cooked.
2. Sauté bell pepper and corn in 1 tbsp oil.
3. Brown turkey in remaining oil. Add taco seasoning and splash water.
4. Mash avocado with lime and salt.
5. Assemble bowls with rice, turkey, veggies, and avocado.
