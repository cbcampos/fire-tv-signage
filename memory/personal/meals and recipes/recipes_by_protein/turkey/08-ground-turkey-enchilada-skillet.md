# 🌮 Ground Turkey Enchilada Skillet

**Serves 6**

### Ingredients

- 1 lb ground turkey
- 1 small onion, diced
- 1 (15-oz) can black beans, drained
- 1 cup corn
- 1 can red enchilada sauce
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- ½ tsp chili powder
- Salt to taste
- 1 cup queso sauce
- 1 cup shredded cheese

### Instructions

1. Brown turkey with onion.
2. Stir in beans, corn, enchilada sauce, cumin, chili powder.
3. Simmer 10 minutes.
4. Stir in queso.
5. Top with shredded cheese and cover until melted.
Serve with chips or tortillas.
