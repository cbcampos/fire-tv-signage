# 🌮 Ground Turkey Taco Salad with Cilantro Lime Rice & Chipotle Crema

**Serves 6**

## 🌮 Taco Turkey

### Ingredients

- 2½ lb ground turkey
- 2 taco seasoning packets
- 1 tbsp olive oil
Brown turkey with seasoning.

## 🍚 Cilantro Lime Rice

- 2 cups rice
- Juice of 2 limes
- ½ cup chopped cilantro
- 1 tbsp olive oil
Cook rice. Stir in lime and cilantro.

## 🌶️ Chipotle Crema

- 1 cup Greek yogurt
- 1 chipotle pepper + 1 tbsp adobo sauce
- 1 tbsp lime juice
- Salt
Blend until smooth.
