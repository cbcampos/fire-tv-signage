# 🌮 Turkey Taco Bowls with Cilantro Lime Rice & Green Chile Crema

**Serves 4–5**

## 🌮 Taco Turkey

### Ingredients

- 1½ lb ground turkey
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 2 tsp chili powder
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp onion powder
- Salt
Brown turkey in oil. Add spices and splash of water. Simmer 3–5 minutes.

## 🍚 Cilantro Lime Rice

### Ingredients

- 1 cup jasmine rice
- 2 cups water
- Juice of 1 lime
- ¼ cup chopped cilantro
- Salt
Cook rice. Stir in lime juice and cilantro.

## 🌶️ Green Chile Crema

### Ingredients

- 2 tbsp canned green chiles
- ¼ cup Greek yogurt
- ¼ cup mayo
- ½ tsp smoked paprika
- Juice of ½ lime
- Salt
Whisk until smooth.
