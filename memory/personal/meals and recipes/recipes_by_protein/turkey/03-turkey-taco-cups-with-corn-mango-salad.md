# 🌮 Turkey Taco Cups with Corn & Mango Salad

**Serves 6**

## 🌮 Turkey Taco Cups

### Ingredients

- 2 lbs ground turkey
- ½ onion, finely diced
- 2 tsp mild taco seasoning
- 12 small soft tortillas
- Olive oil

### Instructions

1. Sauté onion in olive oil.
2. Add turkey and seasoning. Cook until browned.
3. Press tortillas into muffin tin wells.
4. Fill with turkey mixture.
5. Bake at 375°F for 5–7 minutes if crisping desired.

## 🌽 Corn & Mango Salad

### Ingredients

- 2 cups corn
- 2 mangoes, diced
- 1 red bell pepper, diced
- Juice of 1 lime
- 1 tbsp olive oil
Toss all ingredients together and chill.

## 🥣 Cilantro-Lime Yogurt Sauce

### Ingredients

- 1 cup Greek yogurt
- Juice of 1 lime
- ¼ cup chopped cilantro
- ¼ tsp garlic powder
Whisk until smooth.
