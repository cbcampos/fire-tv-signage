# 🌮 Ground Turkey Taco Soup

**Serves 6**

### Ingredients

- 1½ lb ground turkey
- 1½ tbsp olive oil
- 1 medium onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp taco seasoning
- 2 (15-oz) cans black beans, drained
- 1 (15-oz) can corn, drained
- 1 (15-oz) can diced tomatoes
- 1 (15-oz) can tomato sauce
- 3 cups chicken broth

## Optional Toppings

- Diced avocado
- Sour cream
- Shredded cheese

### Instructions

1. Brown turkey with onion in olive oil.
2. Add garlic and taco seasoning.
3. Stir in beans, corn, tomatoes, sauce, and broth.
4. Simmer 20 minutes. Serve with toppings.
