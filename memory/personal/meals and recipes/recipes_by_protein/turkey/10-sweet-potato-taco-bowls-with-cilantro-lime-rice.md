# 🌮 Sweet Potato Taco Bowls with Cilantro Lime Rice

**Serves 6**

## 🍠 Roasted Sweet Potatoes

### Ingredients

- 2 large sweet potatoes, diced
- 1½ tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- ½ tsp cumin
- Salt and pepper
Roast at 425°F for 25–30 minutes.

## 🌮 Turkey Filling

### Ingredients

- 1½ lb ground turkey
- 1 onion, diced
- 1 bell pepper, diced
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 can black beans
- 1 tsp chili powder
- 1 tsp cumin
- ½ tsp paprika
Brown turkey with vegetables and spices. Stir in beans and simmer.

## 🍚 Cilantro Lime Rice

- 1½ cups rice
- 2¼ cups broth
- Zest and juice of 2 limes
- ½ cup chopped cilantro
Cook rice. Stir in lime and cilantro.

## 🥑 Avocado Sauce

- 1 avocado
- Juice of 1 lime
- 2 tbsp Greek yogurt
- Salt
Blend until smooth.
