# 🍝 White Pasta with Turkey Meatballs & Marinara

**Serves 6**

## 🧆 Turkey Meatballs

### Ingredients

- 2 lb ground turkey
- 1 cup breadcrumbs
- ½ cup grated Parmesan
- 1 egg
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp parsley
- 1 tsp salt
- ½ tsp pepper
- 1 tbsp olive oil

### Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
2. Mix ingredients and form about 30 meatballs.
3. Brown in skillet 2–3 minutes per side.
4. Transfer to oven 10–12 minutes until cooked through.

## 🍝 Pasta & Sauce

### Ingredients

- 1½–2 lb white pasta
- 4 cups marinara
Cook pasta in salted water. Warm sauce. Combine pasta, meatballs, and sauce.
