# 🍝 Baked Turkey Meatballs with Spaghetti

**Serves 6**

## 🧆 Turkey Meatballs

### Ingredients

- 2½ lb ground turkey
- 2 eggs
- 1½ cups breadcrumbs
- ½ cup grated Parmesan
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp Italian seasoning
- 1 tsp salt
- ½ tsp black pepper

### Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
2. Mix all ingredients.
3. Form 24 meatballs.
4. Bake 20–25 minutes until cooked through.

## 🍝 Pasta & Sauce

### Ingredients

- 1½ lb spaghetti
- 2 jars marinara sauce
Cook pasta in salted water. Heat marinara and add baked meatballs. Serve together.
