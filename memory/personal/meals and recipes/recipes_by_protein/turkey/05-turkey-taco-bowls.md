# 🌮 Turkey Taco Bowls

**Serves 6**

## 🍚 Cilantro Lime Rice

### Ingredients

- 3 cups uncooked rice
- 6 cups water or broth
- Juice of 2 limes
- Zest of 1 lime
- 1 cup chopped cilantro
- Salt to taste

### Instructions

1. Rinse rice.
2. Cook rice with water and salt.
3. Stir in lime juice, zest, and cilantro after cooking. Fluff and set aside.

## 🌮 Taco Turkey

### Ingredients

- 2½ lb ground turkey
- 3 tbsp taco seasoning
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- ¼ cup water

### Instructions

1. Heat oil and brown turkey.
2. Stir in seasoning and water. Simmer 3–5 minutes.

## 🥑 Avocado Crema

### Ingredients

- 2 avocados
- ½ cup Greek yogurt or sour cream
- Juice of 1 lime
- Salt to taste
Blend until smooth and creamy.

## Optional Bowl Toppings

- 1 can black beans, drained
- 1 cup corn
- Diced tomatoes
- Shredded cheese
Assemble bowls with rice, turkey, beans, corn, toppings, and crema.
