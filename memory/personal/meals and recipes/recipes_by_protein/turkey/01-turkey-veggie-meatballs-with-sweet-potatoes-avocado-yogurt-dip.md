# 🧆 Turkey Veggie Meatballs with Sweet Potatoes & Avocado Yogurt Dip

**Serves 6**

## 🧆 Turkey Veggie Meatballs

### Ingredients

- 2 lbs ground turkey
- 1 cup shredded zucchini, squeezed dry
- 1 cup shredded carrots
- 1 cup finely chopped spinach
- ½ cup breadcrumbs or oat flour
- 2 eggs
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- ½ tsp onion powder
- ½ tsp paprika
- Black pepper to taste
- Olive oil spray

### Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
2. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl until just mixed.
3. Form into 1.5-inch meatballs and place on parchment-lined sheet.
4. Lightly spray with olive oil.
5. Bake 18–20 minutes until internal temp reaches 165°F.

## 🍠 Roasted Sweet Potatoes

### Ingredients

- 3 large sweet potatoes, cut into wedges
- 1–2 tbsp olive oil
- Cinnamon (optional)
- Salt (optional)

### Instructions

1. Toss wedges with olive oil and seasoning.
2. Roast at 400°F for 25–30 minutes, flipping halfway.

## 🥑 Avocado Yogurt Dip

### Ingredients

- 2 ripe avocados
- 1 cup plain Greek yogurt
- Juice of 1 lime
- Pinch cumin
- Salt to taste

### Instructions

Mash or blend until smooth. Chill until serving.
