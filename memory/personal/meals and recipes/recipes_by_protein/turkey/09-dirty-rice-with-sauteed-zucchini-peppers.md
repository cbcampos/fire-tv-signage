# 🍚 Dirty Rice with Sautéed Zucchini & Peppers

**Serves 6**

## 🍚 Dirty Rice

### Ingredients

- 2 boxes Dirty Rice mix
- 1½ lb ground turkey
Prepare mix according to package, adding browned turkey.

## 🥒 Sautéed Vegetables

### Ingredients

- 2 zucchinis, sliced
- 2 bell peppers, sliced
- 1–2 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper
Sauté 6–8 minutes until tender but slightly crisp.
