# 🌭 Conecuh Sausage Dogs with Sauerkraut, Frozen Waffle Fries & Fresh Fruit

**Prep:** 5 min | **Cook:** 20 min | **Serves:** 6

Easy casual weeknight — kids love it. Conecuh sausage links served like hot dogs with sauerkraut on top, oven fries and fruit on the side.

### Ingredients

- 1 (12-14 oz) package Conecuh sausage links (original or hickory smoked)
- 6 hot dog buns
- 1 (16 oz) jar sauerkraut, drained
- 1 bag frozen waffle fries (about 22–32 oz)
- 1 small seedless watermelon, cubed
- Yellow mustard (optional, for dogs)
- Ketchup (optional, for fries)

### Instructions

1. **Fries:** Preheat oven to 425°F (or air fryer to 400°F). Bake waffle fries per package directions, about 18–22 min, flipping halfway.
2. **Sauerkraut:** Drain sauerkraut in a colander, pressing out excess liquid. Warm in small saucepan over medium heat 5 min (or microwave in covered bowl 2 min).
3. **Conecuh links:** Heat a skillet over medium heat. Add Conecuh sausage links and cook 8–10 min, turning frequently, until browned and heated through (internal temp 165°F if using a thermometer).
4. **Fruit:** Cut watermelon into cubes while everything cooks.
5. **Serve:** Build dogs: bun + Conecuh link + sauerkraut on top (+ mustard if you want). Plate with fries and watermelon on the side.
