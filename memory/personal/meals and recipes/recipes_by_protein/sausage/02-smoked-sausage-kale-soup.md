# 🥣 Smoked Sausage & Kale Soup

**Serves 6**

### Ingredients

- 1 lb Conecuh smoked sausage, sliced
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 2 medium yellow onions, diced
- 4 carrots, sliced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 (15-oz) cans cannellini or great northern beans, drained
- 8 cups chicken broth
- 2 lb fresh kale, chopped
- 1 tsp dried thyme
- Salt and black pepper
- Optional red pepper flakes

### Instructions

1. Brown sausage in olive oil.
2. Add onions, carrots, garlic, thyme. Sauté 5 minutes.
3. Add beans and broth. Simmer 10 minutes.
4. Stir in kale and cook 15–20 minutes until tender.
5. Season to taste.
