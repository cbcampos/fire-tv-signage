# 🥣 Creamy Kale, White Bean & Sausage Soup

**Serves 6**

### Ingredients

- 1½ lb smoked sausage, sliced
- 1½ tbsp olive oil
- 1 large onion, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 6 cups chicken broth
- 2 (15-oz) cans white beans, drained
- 3 medium Yukon gold potatoes, cubed
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- ½ tsp black pepper
- 3 cups chopped kale
- ¾ cup heavy cream
- Salt to taste
- French bread for serving

### Instructions

1. Brown sausage in olive oil. Remove and set aside.
2. In same pot, sauté onion and garlic.
3. Add broth, beans, potatoes, paprika, and pepper. Simmer 15 minutes.
4. Add kale and sausage. Simmer 3–4 minutes.
5. Stir in cream and adjust salt. Serve with bread.
