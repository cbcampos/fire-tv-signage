# 🍝 Creamy Tomato Gnocchi with Italian Sausage & Spinach

**Serves 6**

### Ingredients

- 1½ lbs Italian sausage (mild or hot), casings removed
- 1 (16 oz) package shelf-stable or refrigerated gnocchi
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 small onion, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 (28 oz) can crushed tomatoes
- 1 cup heavy cream (or half-and-half)
- ½ tsp dried basil
- ½ tsp dried oregano
- ½ tsp red pepper flakes (optional)
- 5 oz fresh baby spinach
- ½ cup grated Parmesan
- Salt and pepper to taste

### Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in large skillet over medium-high. Add Italian sausage (casings removed), breaking up with a spoon. Cook 6–8 min until browned. Transfer to a plate.
2. In same skillet, add onion. Cook 3–4 min until softened. Add garlic, cook 1 min until fragrant.
3. Pour in crushed tomatoes, heavy cream, basil, oregano, red pepper flakes. Stir to combine. Simmer 5–7 min to thicken.
4. Add gnocchi directly to the sauce (no need to pre-boil). Simmer 8–10 min, stirring occasionally, until gnocchi is tender and sauce has thickened.
5. Stir in spinach until wilted. Return sausage to pan. Stir in Parmesan. Season with salt and pepper.
6. Serve hot with extra Parmesan on top.
