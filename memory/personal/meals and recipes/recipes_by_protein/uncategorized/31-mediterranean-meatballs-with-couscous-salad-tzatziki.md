# 🧆 Mediterranean Meatballs with Couscous, Salad & Tzatziki

**Serves 6**

## 🧆 Turkey Meatballs

### Ingredients

- 1½ lb ground turkey
- ½ cup breadcrumbs
- 1 egg
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- ½ tsp cumin
- 1 tsp salt
- ½ tsp black pepper

### Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
2. Mix all ingredients until just combined.
3. Form into 18–24 meatballs.
4. Bake 20–25 minutes until internal temp reaches 165°F.

## 🍚 Couscous

### Ingredients

- 1½ cups dry couscous
- 2 cups broth or water
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt to taste
Bring liquid to boil. Stir in couscous, cover, remove from heat 5 minutes. Fluff with fork.

## 🥗 Cucumber Tomato Salad

### Ingredients

- 1 cucumber, diced
- 2 tomatoes, diced
- Juice of ½ lemon
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 2 tbsp chopped parsley
- Salt and pepper
Toss and chill.

## 🥣 Tzatziki

### Ingredients

- 1 cup Greek yogurt
- ½ cucumber, grated and squeezed dry
- 1 garlic clove, minced
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- ½ tsp dried dill
- Salt to taste
Mix and chill.
