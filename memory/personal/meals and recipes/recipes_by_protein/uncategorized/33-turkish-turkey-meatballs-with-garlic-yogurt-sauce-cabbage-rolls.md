# 🧆 Turkish Turkey Meatballs with Garlic Yogurt Sauce & Cabbage Rolls

**Serves 6**

## 🧆 Turkish Meatballs

### Ingredients

- 2½ lb ground turkey
- ¼ cup breadcrumbs
- 1½ onions, grated
- 3 garlic cloves
- 2 eggs
- 1½ cups parsley
- 2 tsp cumin
- 2 tsp coriander
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp chili flakes
- Salt and pepper
Mix and form ~30 meatballs. Bake at 425°F for 18–20 minutes.

## 🥣 Garlic Yogurt Sauce

### Ingredients

- 2½ cups Greek yogurt
- 1½ tbsp cornstarch + 3 tbsp water
- 2 eggs
- 1½ cups chicken broth
- Salt and pepper
- Olive oil + Aleppo pepper for drizzle
Simmer broth. Whisk yogurt, eggs, slurry. Slowly temper into broth. Cook until thickened.

## 🥬 Cabbage Rolls

### Ingredients

- 1 large cabbage
- 2 lb ground turkey
- 1½ cups cooked rice
- 1 can diced tomatoes, drained
- 1 onion
- 2 garlic cloves
- ½ cup parsley
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp paprika
- Salt and pepper
- 1 (28-oz) can crushed tomatoes
- 1 cup broth
Blanch cabbage leaves. Mix filling. Roll and arrange in dish. Cover with sauce. Bake at 350°F for 45–55 minutes.
