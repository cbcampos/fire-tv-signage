# 🔥 Boneless Buffalo Chicken with Caesar Salad

**Serves 6**

## 🔥 Buffalo Chicken

### Ingredients

- 2½ lb chicken breast or thighs, cut into chunks
- 3 eggs, beaten
- 1½ cups panko breadcrumbs
- Salt, black pepper, paprika
- ¾ cup buffalo sauce
- 2 tbsp melted butter (if using hot sauce base)

### Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
2. Season chicken lightly with salt, pepper, and paprika.
3. Dip pieces in egg, then coat in panko.
4. Arrange on lined baking sheet.
5. Bake 20–25 minutes until golden and internal temp reaches 165°F.
6. Warm buffalo sauce (mix with melted butter if needed).
7. Toss baked chicken in sauce just before serving.

## 🥗 Caesar Salad

### Ingredients

- 2 heads romaine, chopped
- ¾ cup Caesar dressing
- ½ cup croutons
- ½ cup grated Parmesan
Toss and serve alongside chicken.
