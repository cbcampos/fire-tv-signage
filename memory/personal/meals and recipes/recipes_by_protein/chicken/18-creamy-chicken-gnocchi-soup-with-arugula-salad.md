# 🥣 Creamy Chicken Gnocchi Soup with Arugula Salad

**Serves 6**

## 🥣 Creamy Chicken Gnocchi Soup

### Ingredients

- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 yellow onion, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 3 carrots, diced
- 3 celery stalks, chopped
- 2 lb chicken breast, cooked and shredded
- 1 tsp dried thyme
- 6 cups low-sodium chicken broth
- 2 (16 oz) packages potato gnocchi
- 2 cups baby spinach
- 1½ cups half-and-half or whole milk
- Salt and black pepper to taste
- ¼ cup grated Parmesan (optional)

### Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in large pot over medium heat.
2. Add onion, garlic, carrots, and celery. Sauté 5–7 minutes until softened.
3. Stir in thyme.
4. Add broth and shredded chicken. Bring to simmer.
5. Add gnocchi and cook until they float, about 3–4 minutes.
6. Stir in milk and spinach. Simmer 5–10 minutes.
7. Season with salt, pepper, and Parmesan if using.

## 🥗 Arugula Salad

### Ingredients

- 6 cups arugula
- Juice of 1 lemon
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper
- Shaved Parmesan (optional)
Toss just before serving.
