# 🥣 White Chicken Chili with Cornbread

**Serves 6**

## 🌶️ White Chicken Chili

### Ingredients

- 1½ lb shredded chicken
- 2 (15-oz) cans white beans
- 1 (4-oz) can green chiles
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 garlic cloves
- 3 cups chicken broth
- 1 tsp cumin
- ½ tsp oregano
- Salt and pepper
- ½ cup Greek yogurt or sour cream

### Instructions

1. Sauté onion and garlic.
2. Add beans, chiles, broth, chicken, and spices.
3. Simmer 20 minutes.
4. Stir in yogurt and heat gently.

## 🍞 Cornbread

### Ingredients

- 1 cup cornmeal
- 1 cup flour
- 1 tbsp baking powder
- ½ tsp salt
- ⅓ cup sugar
- 1 cup milk
- 2 eggs
- ⅓ cup oil or melted butter
Mix dry ingredients. Stir in wet. Bake at 400°F for 20–25 minutes.
