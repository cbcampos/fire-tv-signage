# 🍜 Ground Chicken Bowls with Peanut Sauce & Broccoli

**Serves 6**

## 🍗 Bowl Base

### Ingredients

- 3 lb ground chicken
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 3 garlic cloves
- 2 tbsp grated ginger
- 2 bell peppers
- 3 carrots
- 1½–2 lb broccoli
- 1½ cups jasmine rice (uncooked)
- ½ cup peanuts
Cook rice. Brown chicken with sesame oil, garlic, ginger. Add vegetables.

## 🥜 Peanut Sauce

### Ingredients

- ¾ cup peanut butter
- 6 tbsp soy sauce
- 2 tbsp rice vinegar
- 2 tbsp honey
- Juice of 2 limes
- ½ cup warm water
Whisk until smooth. Toss with chicken mixture.
