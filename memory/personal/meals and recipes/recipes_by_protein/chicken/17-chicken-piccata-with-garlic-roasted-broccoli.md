# 🍋 Chicken Piccata with Garlic Roasted Broccoli

**Serves 6**

## 🍗 Chicken Piccata

### Ingredients

- 3 lb chicken breasts, sliced into cutlets
- ½ cup flour
- Salt and pepper
- 4 tbsp olive oil
- 4 tbsp butter
- 4 cloves garlic
- 1 cup chicken broth
- ⅔ cup fresh lemon juice
- ¼ cup capers
- ¼ cup parsley

### Instructions

1. Dredge seasoned chicken in flour.
2. Sear in olive oil until golden. Remove.
3. Melt butter in pan. Add garlic.
4. Stir in broth, lemon juice, and capers. Simmer 5–7 minutes.
5. Return chicken and spoon sauce over. Garnish with parsley.

## 🥦 Garlic Roasted Broccoli

### Ingredients

- 2 heads broccoli
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 4 cloves garlic
- Salt and pepper
Roast at 425°F for 20 minutes.
