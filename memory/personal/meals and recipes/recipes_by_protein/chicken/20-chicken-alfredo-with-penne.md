# 🍝 Chicken Alfredo with Penne (from scratch)

**Serves 6** (makes plenty of leftovers for Wednesday)

## 🍗 Chicken

### Ingredients

- 2½ lbs chicken breast, cut into bite-size pieces
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp Italian seasoning
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- Salt and black pepper

### Instructions

1. Pat chicken dry. Toss with olive oil, Italian seasoning, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
2. Heat large skillet over medium-high. Add chicken in a single layer (work in batches if needed).
3. Cook 4–5 minutes per side until golden brown and internal temp reaches 165°F.
4. Transfer to a plate and tent with foil to keep warm.

## 🍝 Penne

### Ingredients

- 1.5 lbs penne pasta
- 1 tbsp salt (for pasta water)

### Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of water to a rolling boil. Add salt.
2. Cook penne until al dente per package directions (about 11 minutes).
3. **Reserve 1 cup pasta water** before draining. Drain and set aside.

## 🧀 Alfredo Sauce (from scratch)

### Ingredients

- 6 tbsp unsalted butter
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1½ cups heavy cream
- 1½ cups whole milk
- 1¼ cups freshly grated Parmesan (pre-grated works in a pinch)
- ¼ tsp ground nutmeg
- Salt and black pepper to taste

### Instructions

1. In the same skillet used for chicken, melt butter over medium heat.
2. Add garlic and cook 60–90 seconds until fragrant (don't let it brown).
3. Pour in cream and milk. Bring to a gentle simmer (don't boil).
4. Whisk in Parmesan a handful at a time until fully melted and smooth.
5. Add nutmeg. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
6. Simmer 2–3 minutes to thicken slightly. If sauce gets too thick, splash in reserved pasta water.

## 🍽️ Combine & Serve

1. Add drained penne and chicken (plus any juices) to the sauce.
2. Toss gently to coat. Add pasta water a splash at a time if needed for a silky consistency.
3. Serve immediately. Garnish with extra Parmesan and cracked pepper.

## 🔥 Reheating Leftovers (Wednesday)

- Add leftover Alfredo to a skillet over low heat with a splash of milk.
- Stir gently until warmed through and creamy again. Don't microwave on high — sauce can break.
- Pair with Caesar salad kit from the store.
