# 🍛 Red Curry Coconut Chicken Soup

**Serves 6**

### Ingredients

- 1½–2 lb chicken thighs, cubed
- 1 tbsp oil
- 1 onion, sliced
- 1 red bell pepper, sliced
- 4 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tbsp ginger
- 3 tbsp red curry paste
- 2 (13.5-oz) cans coconut milk
- 4 cups chicken broth
- 1 tbsp fish sauce
- Juice of 1 lime
- 1½ cups jasmine rice (dry), cooked

### Instructions

1. Cook rice separately.
2. Sauté onion, bell pepper, garlic, and ginger in oil.
3. Stir in curry paste.
4. Add coconut milk, broth, and chicken. Simmer 15–20 minutes.
5. Stir in lime juice and fish sauce.
6. Serve over rice.
