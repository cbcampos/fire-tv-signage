# 🍜 Teriyaki Chicken & Veggie Stir-Fry with Rice

**Serves 4–5**

## 🍗 Teriyaki Chicken

### Ingredients

- 2 lb chicken breasts, sliced
- 1 tbsp oil
- Salt and pepper
- ½ cup teriyaki sauce
Sauté chicken in oil until cooked through. Stir in teriyaki sauce and simmer 3–4 minutes.

## 🥦 Vegetables

- 1 head broccoli
- 2 carrots
- 1 bell pepper
- 1 tbsp oil
Sauté until tender crisp.

## 🍚 Rice

- 1 cup jasmine rice
- 2 cups water
Cook and serve with chicken and vegetables.
