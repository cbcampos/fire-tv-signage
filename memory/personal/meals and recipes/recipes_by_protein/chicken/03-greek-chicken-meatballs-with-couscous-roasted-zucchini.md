# 🥙 Greek Chicken Meatballs with Couscous & Roasted Zucchini

**Serves 6**

## 🧆 Greek Chicken Meatballs

### Ingredients

- 2¼ lb ground chicken
- ¾ cup breadcrumbs
- 2 eggs
- ½ cup chopped parsley
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- ½ tsp black pepper
- ½ tsp salt

### Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
2. Mix all ingredients until just combined.
3. Form about 24 meatballs and place on lined baking sheet.
4. Bake 18–20 minutes until internal temperature reaches 165°F.

## 🥒 Roasted Zucchini

### Ingredients

- 3 zucchini, chopped
- 1½ tbsp olive oil
- ¼ tsp black pepper
- Salt to taste
Toss and roast at 425°F for 15–20 minutes.

## 🍚 Couscous

Prepare 2 boxes couscous according to package directions.

## 🥣 Tzatziki

### Ingredients

- 2 cups Greek yogurt
- 1 cucumber, grated and drained
- 1 garlic clove, minced
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- ½ tsp salt
Mix and chill before serving.
