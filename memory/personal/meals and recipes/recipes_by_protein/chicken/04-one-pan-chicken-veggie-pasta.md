# 🍝 One-Pan Chicken & Veggie Pasta

**Serves 6**

### Ingredients

- 2¼ lb diced chicken breast
- 1½ lb small pasta
- 2 zucchini, chopped
- 2 carrots, grated
- 3 cups baby spinach
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 3 cups marinara
- 4 cups low-sodium chicken broth
- ½ tsp black pepper
- Salt to taste

### Instructions

1. Heat 2 tbsp oil and brown chicken. Remove.
2. Add remaining oil and sauté garlic, zucchini, carrots.
3. Return chicken. Add pasta, sauce, and broth.
4. Simmer 10–12 minutes until pasta tender.
5. Stir in spinach and cook 2–3 minutes more.
