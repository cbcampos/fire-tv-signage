# 🍚 Chicken & Veggie Fried Rice with Steamed Edamame

**Serves 6**

## 🍗 Chicken Fried Rice

### Ingredients

- 2 lbs chicken breast, diced
- 6 cups cooked rice (day-old preferred)
- 4 eggs, beaten
- 1½ cups diced carrots
- 1½ cups peas
- 1 bell pepper, finely chopped
- 2 green onions, sliced
- 3 tbsp olive or avocado oil
- ¼ cup coconut aminos or low-sodium soy sauce

### Instructions

1. Heat 1½ tbsp oil in large skillet. Cook chicken until browned. Remove.
2. Add remaining oil and sauté vegetables 5–6 minutes.
3. Push veggies aside. Scramble eggs.
4. Add rice and chicken back in.
5. Stir in coconut aminos. Cook 5–8 minutes until heated through.

## 🌱 Steamed Edamame

### Ingredients

- 3 cups shelled edamame
Steam 5–6 minutes until tender.
