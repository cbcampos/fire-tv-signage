# White Chicken Chili

**Serves 6 adults**

## Ingredients

- 2 cans (15 oz) great northern beans, drained
- 1 can (15 oz) corn, drained
- 1 can (4 oz) diced green chiles
- 1 lb cooked chicken breast, shredded (rotisserie works)
- 1 small white onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 cups chicken broth
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp oregano
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper to taste

### Jalapeño Cornbread
- 1 box (8.5 oz) cornbread mix
- 1 cup corn kernels (canned or frozen, thawed)
- 1 fresh jalapeño, finely diced
- ½ cup buttermilk
- 1 egg

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Sauté onion 4–5 min until soft. Add garlic, cook 1 min.
2. Add beans, corn, chiles, chicken, broth, cumin, and oregano. Simmer 15–20 min. Season with salt and pepper.
3. Preheat oven per cornbread mix directions. Mix cornbread batter with corn, jalapeño, buttermilk, and egg. Bake per box time.
4. Serve chili with cornbread on the side.
