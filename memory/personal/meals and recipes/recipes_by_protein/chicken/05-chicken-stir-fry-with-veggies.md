# 🥢 Chicken Stir-Fry with Veggies

**Serves 6**

## 🍗 Stir-Fry

### Ingredients

- 3 lb chicken breast, sliced
- 3 bell peppers, sliced
- 3 cups broccoli florets
- 3 carrots, sliced
- 1 onion, sliced
- 4 garlic cloves, minced
- 3 tbsp oil
- 4 cups cooked jasmine rice

## 🥣 Sauce

- ½ cup soy sauce
- 3 tbsp honey or brown sugar
- 2 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tbsp grated ginger
- 1 tbsp cornstarch + 3 tbsp water

### Instructions

1. Stir-fry chicken in oil. Remove.
2. Sauté garlic and vegetables until crisp tender.
3. Return chicken. Add sauce.
4. Stir in cornstarch slurry and cook until thickened.
5. Serve over rice.
