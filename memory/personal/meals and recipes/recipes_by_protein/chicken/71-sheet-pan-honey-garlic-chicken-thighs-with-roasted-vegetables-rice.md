# 🍗 Sheet Pan Honey Garlic Chicken Thighs with Roasted Vegetables & Rice

**Serves 6**

### Ingredients

- Chicken:
- 2½ lbs bone-in chicken thighs (skin-on for crispiness, about 6-8 thighs)
- Salt and pepper
- Honey Garlic Sauce:
- ⅓ cup honey
- 3 tbsp soy sauce (low-sodium)
- 2 tbsp rice vinegar
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp grated fresh ginger
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- Roasted Vegetables:
- 1 large head broccoli, cut into florets
- 2 bell peppers (any color), sliced
- 2 carrots, sliced into rounds
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper
- Rice:
- 2 cups jasmine or long-grain white rice
- 3½ cups water
- 1 tsp salt

### Instructions

1. **Sauce:** Whisk honey, soy sauce, rice vinegar, garlic, ginger, sesame oil in a small bowl. Set aside.
2. **Chicken:** Preheat oven to 425°F. Pat chicken thighs dry, season generously with salt and pepper. Place skin-side up on one side of a large rimmed sheet pan.
3. **Veggies:** In a large bowl, toss broccoli, bell peppers, carrots with olive oil, salt, pepper. Spread on the other side of the sheet pan (in a single layer if possible).
4. **Bake:** Brush half the honey garlic sauce over chicken thighs. Roast 25-30 min (chicken should reach 175°F internal and skin should be crispy), brushing with more sauce at the halfway point. Stir veggies halfway through so they cook evenly.
5. **Rice:** While sheet pan roasts, rinse rice, combine with water and salt in a saucepan. Bring to boil, reduce to low, cover, simmer 15 min. Rest 5 min, fluff with fork.
6. **Serve:** Plate rice, top with chicken thighs and roasted veggies. Drizzle remaining honey garlic sauce over everything.
