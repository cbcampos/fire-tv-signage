# 🍔 Aldi Chicken Sandwiches with Special Sauce & Air-Fried Sides

**Serves 6**

## 🍗 Chicken Sandwiches

### Ingredients

- 6 breaded chicken cutlets
- 6 potato buns
- Dill pickle slices
Bake chicken according to package instructions. Toast buns.

## 🌟 Special Sauce

- ¾ cup mayo
- ⅓ cup Dijon mustard
- ¼ cup pickle brine
- ½ tsp garlic powder
- ½ tsp smoked paprika
Mix until smooth.

## 🍠 Sweet Potato Fries

- 3 sweet potatoes or frozen fries
- Olive oil, salt, paprika
Air fry at 400°F for 15–20 minutes.

## 🥦 Air-Fried Green Beans

- 1½ lb green beans
- Olive oil, garlic powder
Air fry 10–12 minutes at 400°F.
