# 🥙 Mediterranean Chicken & Chickpea Pita Wraps

**Serves 6**

## 🍗 Chicken & Chickpeas

### Ingredients

- 3 lb cooked chicken (grilled or rotisserie), chopped
- 2 (15-oz) cans chickpeas, drained and rinsed
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- ½ tsp garlic powder
- Salt and pepper to taste

### Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in skillet.
2. Add chicken and chickpeas.
3. Season with oregano, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
4. Warm 5–7 minutes until heated through.

## 🥣 Lemon Yogurt Sauce

### Ingredients

- 1 cup Greek yogurt
- Juice of 1–2 lemons
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tsp oregano
- Salt and pepper
Whisk until smooth and chill.

## 🥙 Assembly

## Additional Ingredients

- 6 pita breads
- 3 cucumbers, diced
- 3 tomatoes, diced
- ¾ cup feta (optional)
Warm pitas. Fill with chicken mixture, vegetables, feta, and drizzle with sauce.
