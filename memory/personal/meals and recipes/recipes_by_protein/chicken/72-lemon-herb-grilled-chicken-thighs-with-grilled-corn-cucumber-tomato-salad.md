# 🥗 Lemon Herb Grilled Chicken Thighs with Grilled Corn & Cucumber Tomato Salad

**Serves 6**

### Ingredients

- Chicken:
- 2½ lbs boneless skinless chicken thighs (about 8-10 thighs)
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 3 tbsp fresh lemon juice (1-2 lemons)
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp fresh oregano (or 1 tsp dried)
- 1 tsp salt
- ½ tsp black pepper
- Grilled Corn:
- 6 ears corn, husked
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Salt to taste
- Cucumber Tomato Salad:
- 1 English cucumber, diced
- 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
- ½ small red onion, thinly sliced
- ½ cup crumbled feta (optional)
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 2 tbsp red wine vinegar
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- Salt and pepper
- 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley or dill

### Instructions

1. **Marinate chicken:** Whisk olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, oregano, salt, pepper in a large bowl. Add chicken thighs, toss to coat. Marinate at least 30 min in fridge (up to 4 hours).
2. **Grill chicken:** Preheat grill to medium-high (or grill pan on stovetop). Grill chicken 5-6 min per side until internal temp reaches 165°F. Rest 3 min before serving.
3. **Grill corn:** Brush corn with olive oil, sprinkle with salt. Grill 8-10 min, turning, until lightly charred all over.
4. **Salad:** Combine cucumber, tomatoes, red onion, feta in a large bowl. Whisk olive oil, vinegar, oregano, salt, pepper. Pour over salad. Toss. Top with fresh parsley.
5. **Serve:** Plate grilled chicken with corn on the cob and salad on the side.
