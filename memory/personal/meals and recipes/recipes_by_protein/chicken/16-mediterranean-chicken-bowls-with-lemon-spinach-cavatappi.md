# 🥙 Mediterranean Chicken Bowls with Lemon Spinach Cavatappi

**Serves 4–5**

## 🍗 Garlic Herb Chicken

### Ingredients

- 2 lb chicken breasts, sliced
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Juice of 1 lemon
- 3 garlic cloves
- 1 tsp oregano
- Salt and pepper
Marinate 10–20 minutes. Sauté until cooked through.

## 🍝 Lemon Spinach Cavatappi

### Ingredients

- 2 cups dry cavatappi
- 1–2 tbsp olive oil
- Juice of 1 lemon
- 1 garlic clove
- 2 cups spinach
- Salt and pepper
- Optional cherry tomatoes or feta
Cook pasta. Toss with oil, lemon, garlic, spinach.

## 🥣 Tzatziki

- ¾ cup Greek yogurt
- ½ cucumber
- 1 garlic clove
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- ½ tsp dill
- Salt
Mix and chill.
