# 🍜 Asian Garlic Noodle Stir-Fry with Chicken

**Serves 6**

## 🍜 Noodles & Chicken

### Ingredients

- 2 lb chicken breast or thighs, thinly sliced
- 2 tbsp cornstarch
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 tbsp neutral oil
- 4 garlic cloves, minced
- ½ head green cabbage, shredded
- 2 carrots, julienned
- 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
- 1 lb lo mein, yakisoba, or rice noodles

## 🥣 Sauce

- ⅓ cup low-sodium soy sauce
- 2 tbsp hoisin sauce
- 1 tbsp honey or brown sugar
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tsp grated ginger
- Chili flakes (optional)

### Instructions

1. Cook noodles according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
2. Toss sliced chicken with cornstarch.
3. Heat neutral oil and sesame oil in large skillet or wok.
4. Sear chicken until golden and cooked through. Remove.
5. Add garlic and vegetables. Stir-fry 3–5 minutes until tender-crisp.
6. Return chicken to pan.
7. Add noodles and sauce. Toss well to coat and heat through.
