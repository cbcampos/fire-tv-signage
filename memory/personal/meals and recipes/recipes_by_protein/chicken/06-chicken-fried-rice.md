# 🍚 Chicken Fried Rice

**Serves 6**

## 🍳 Fried Rice

### Ingredients

- 4 cups cooked rice
- 2 cups cooked chicken, diced
- 6 eggs
- 1 onion, diced
- 1 cup frozen peas and carrots
- 3–4 tbsp soy sauce
- 2 tbsp sesame oil
- Green onions (optional)

### Instructions

1. Scramble eggs in skillet and remove.
2. Sauté onion and vegetables.
3. Add rice and chicken. Stir-fry.
4. Stir in soy sauce and sesame oil.
5. Add eggs back in and mix well.
