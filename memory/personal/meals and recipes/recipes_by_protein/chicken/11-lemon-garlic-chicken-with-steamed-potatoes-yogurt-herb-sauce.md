# 🍋 Lemon Garlic Chicken with Steamed Potatoes & Yogurt Herb Sauce

**Serves 6**

## 🍗 Lemon Garlic Chicken

### Ingredients

- 6 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- Zest of 1 lemon
- Juice of 2 lemons
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp dried oregano
- 1½ tsp salt
- ½ tsp black pepper
- 1 tbsp smoked paprika (optional)
- ½ tsp sugar (optional)

### Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
2. Mix garlic, lemon juice, zest, oil, oregano, salt, pepper, and optional spices.
3. Toss chicken in marinade. Let rest 10–20 minutes if time allows.
4. Roast 25–30 minutes or until internal temp reaches 165°F.
5. Rest 5 minutes before slicing.

## 🥔 Steamed Potatoes

### Ingredients

- 2¼ lb white or Yukon gold potatoes
- Salt
Peel and cut into chunks. Steam or boil 15–20 minutes until fork tender. Season lightly.

## 🥣 Yogurt Herb Sauce

### Ingredients

- 1 cup Greek yogurt
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- ½ tsp Dijon mustard
- 1 tsp honey (optional)
- Salt and pepper
- 2 tbsp chopped parsley or chives
Whisk until smooth and chill until serving.
