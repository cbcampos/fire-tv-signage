# 🍠 Ground Chicken Sweet Potato Hash with Tomato Yogurt Sauce

**Serves 6**

## 🍠 Hash

### Ingredients

- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 3 large sweet potatoes, peeled and diced
- 1 large onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 lb ground chicken
- 1 tbsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- Salt and pepper

### Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in large skillet.
2. Sauté onion and garlic 3–4 minutes.
3. Add sweet potatoes and spices. Cook 10–12 minutes, stirring occasionally.
4. Add ground chicken and cook until browned and fully cooked.
5. Season to taste.

## 🍅 Tomato Yogurt Sauce

### Ingredients

- 1 cup tomato sauce
- ½ cup Greek yogurt or sour cream
- 1 tsp cumin
- ½ tsp smoked paprika
- Salt and garlic powder
Warm gently in saucepan and stir until smooth.
