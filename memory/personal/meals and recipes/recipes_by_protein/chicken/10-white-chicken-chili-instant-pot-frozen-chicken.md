# 🥣 White Chicken Chili (Instant Pot, Frozen Chicken)

**Serves 6**

## 🌶️ Chili

### Ingredients

- 3½–4 lb frozen chicken breasts
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 onion, diced
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 3 (15-oz) cans white beans, rinsed
- 2 (4.5-oz) cans green chiles
- 8 cups chicken broth
- 2 tsp cumin
- 2 tsp dried oregano
- Salt and pepper
- 4–6 oz cream cheese or ½ cup sour cream (optional)

### Instructions

1. Set Instant Pot to sauté. Add olive oil, onion, and garlic. Cook 3–4 minutes.
2. Add frozen chicken, beans, chiles, broth, and spices.
3. Pressure cook on High for 20 minutes.
4. Natural release 10 minutes, then quick release.
5. Remove chicken, shred, and return to pot.
6. Stir in cream cheese or sour cream if using.

## Optional Toppings

- Avocado
- Cilantro
- Shredded cheese
- Lime wedges
