# Spaghetti and Meatballs

**Serves 6 adults**

## Ingredients

- 1 lb dried spaghetti
- 1 jar (32 oz) store-bought marinara
- 1 bag (16 oz) frozen meatballs
- Grated parmesan for serving

## Instructions

1. Bring large pot salted water to boil. Cook spaghetti per package directions until al dente.
2. While pasta boils, warm meatballs in marinara in a saucepan over medium heat, 10–12 min.
3. Reserve ½ cup pasta water, drain spaghetti.
4. Toss spaghetti with meatballs and marinara. Add pasta water if needed to loosen.
5. Serve with parmesan.
