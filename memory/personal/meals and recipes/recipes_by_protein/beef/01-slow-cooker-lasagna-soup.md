# 🍲 Slow Cooker Lasagna Soup

**Serves 6**

## 🥣 Lasagna Soup

### Ingredients

- 1 lb lean ground beef
- 1 small onion
- 5 cloves garlic
- 4 cups broth
- 2 cups marinara
- 1 (15-oz) can diced tomatoes
- 1 tsp basil
- 1 tsp parsley
- ½ tsp oregano
- ¼ tsp rosemary
- 8 lasagna noodles, broken
- 1 tsp balsamic vinegar
- ½ cup Parmesan
- 1 cup mozzarella
- ½ cup ricotta

### Instructions

1. Brown beef and drain.
2. Add all ingredients except noodles and cheeses to slow cooker.
3. Cook Low 6–8 hours or High 3–4 hours.
4. Add noodles 30 minutes before serving.
5. Stir in vinegar and Parmesan.
6. Serve topped with mozzarella and ricotta.
