# Korean Ground Beef

**Serves 6 adults**

## Ingredients

- 1½ lb ground beef
- ½ cup soy sauce
- 2 tbsp brown sugar
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp fresh ginger, grated
- 1 tsp chili flake (optional)
- 2 cups steamed white rice, for serving

### Quick Pickled Cucumbers
- 1 English cucumber, thinly sliced
- 2 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- Pinch of sugar and salt

## Instructions

1. Brown ground beef in a large skillet over medium-high heat, breaking into crumbles. Drain excess fat.
2. Add soy sauce, brown sugar, sesame oil, garlic, ginger, and chili flake. Simmer 5–7 min until sauce thickens slightly.
3. Toss cucumber slices with rice vinegar, sesame oil, sugar, and salt. Let quick-pickle while beef cooks.
4. Serve beef over steamed rice, top with pickled cucumbers.
