# 🌮 Beef Tacos with Spanish Rice & Black Beans

**Serves 6**

### Ingredients

- Taco Filling:
- 1½ lbs ground beef (85/15)
- 1 packet taco seasoning (or DIY: 1 tbsp chili powder, 1 tsp cumin, 1 tsp paprika, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp onion powder, ½ tsp salt, ½ tsp oregano)
- ½ cup water
- 12 hard or soft taco shells
- Spanish Rice:
- 1 cup long-grain white rice
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- ¼ cup diced onion
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 cups chicken broth
- 1 (8 oz) can tomato sauce
- 1 tsp cumin
- ½ tsp salt
- Black Beans:
- 2 (15 oz) cans black beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- ½ tsp cumin
- ½ tsp garlic powder
- Salt to taste
- Toppings Bar:
- 2 cups shredded lettuce
- 2 cups shredded cheddar or Mexican blend cheese
- 2 tomatoes, diced
- 1 cup sour cream
- 1 cup salsa
- 2 limes, cut into wedges
- ½ cup fresh cilantro, chopped
- 2-3 ripe avocados, sliced (optional)
- 1 jalapeño, sliced (optional)

### Instructions

1. **Taco meat:** Heat large skillet over medium-high. Add ground beef, break up, cook 7-8 min until browned. Drain fat. Add taco seasoning and water. Simmer 5-10 min, stirring.
2. **Spanish rice:** Heat olive oil in saucepan over medium. Add rice, toast 2 min until lightly golden. Add onion and garlic, cook 2 min. Add broth, tomato sauce, cumin, salt. Bring to boil, reduce to low, cover, simmer 20 min. Remove from heat, rest 5 min, fluff with fork.
3. **Black beans:** Heat olive oil in small saucepan. Add beans, cumin, garlic powder, salt. Simmer 5-10 min, lightly mash some for thicker texture.
4. **Prep toppings:** While everything cooks, shred lettuce, dice tomatoes, slice limes, chop cilantro.
5. **Assemble:** Build tacos with shells + meat + toppings. Serve rice and beans on the side.
