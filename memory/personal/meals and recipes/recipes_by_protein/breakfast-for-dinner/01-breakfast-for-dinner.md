# 🍳 Breakfast for Dinner

**Serves 6**

## 🍳 Omelettes

### Ingredients

- 14 large eggs
- ½ cup milk
- Salt and pepper
- 1½ cups shredded cheese
- 1½ cups diced ham
- 2 cups fresh spinach
- 4 oz goat cheese
- Butter for cooking

### Instructions

1. Whisk eggs, milk, salt, and pepper.
2. Melt butter in skillet.
3. Pour eggs and cook gently.
4. Add fillings and fold.

## 🥣 Creamy Grits

### Ingredients

- 1½ cups quick grits
- 6 cups water or milk
- 2 tbsp butter
- Salt to taste

### Instructions

1. Bring liquid to boil.
2. Stir in grits and cook according to package.
3. Stir in butter and salt.

## 🍓 Fresh Fruit

- Strawberries, quartered
- Grapes, halved
