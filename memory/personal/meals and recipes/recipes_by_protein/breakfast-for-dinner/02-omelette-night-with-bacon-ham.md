# 🍳 Omelette Night with Bacon & Ham

**Serves 6**

## 🍳 Omelettes

### Ingredients

- 12 eggs
- 6 slices bacon
- ½ lb diced ham
- 1–1½ cups shredded cheese
- ½ onion, diced
- ½ bell pepper, diced
- Salt and pepper
Cook bacon and ham. Sauté vegetables. Cook omelettes and fill as desired.
Serve with toast and fresh fruit.
