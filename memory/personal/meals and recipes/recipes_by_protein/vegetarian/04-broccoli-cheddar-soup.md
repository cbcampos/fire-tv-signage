# 🥣 Broccoli Cheddar Soup

**Serves 6**

## 🧀 Soup

### Ingredients

- 4 tbsp butter
- 1 onion, diced
- 3 carrots, sliced
- 4 cups broccoli florets
- 4 cups broth
- ¼ cup flour
- 1½ cups milk
- 3 cups shredded cheddar
- Salt and pepper
- Pinch nutmeg (optional)

### Instructions

1. Melt butter in pot. Sauté onion and carrots 5 minutes.
2. Add broccoli and broth. Simmer 10–12 minutes.
3. Mix flour with milk to form slurry. Stir into soup.
4. Add cheese and stir until melted.
5. Blend partially if desired.
Serve with crusty bread.
