# 🍝 Spinach & Ricotta Stuffed Shells with Broccoli

**Serves 6**

## 🍝 Stuffed Shells

### Ingredients

- 24 jumbo pasta shells
- 2 cups ricotta
- 2 cups cooked spinach, squeezed dry
- ½ cup Parmesan (optional)
- 1 egg
- 2 cups marinara sauce

### Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
2. Boil shells until al dente; rinse in cold water.
3. Mix ricotta, spinach, Parmesan, egg.
4. Spread 1 cup marinara in baking dish.
5. Stuff shells and arrange in dish.
6. Top with remaining sauce.
7. Cover and bake 20 minutes.

## 🥦 Steamed Broccoli

- 3 broccoli crowns, cut into florets
Steam 7–10 minutes until fork tender.
