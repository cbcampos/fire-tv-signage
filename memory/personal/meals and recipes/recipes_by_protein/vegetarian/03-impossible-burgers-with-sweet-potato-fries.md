# 🍔 Impossible Burgers with Sweet Potato Fries

**Serves 6**

### Ingredients

- 6 Impossible patties
- 6 whole grain buns
- 1 bag frozen sweet potato fries
- Optional: lettuce, tomato, ketchup

### Instructions

1. Bake fries at 425°F for 20–25 minutes.
2. Cook patties in skillet or grill 3–4 minutes per side.
3. Toast buns if desired.
4. Assemble burgers and serve with fries.
