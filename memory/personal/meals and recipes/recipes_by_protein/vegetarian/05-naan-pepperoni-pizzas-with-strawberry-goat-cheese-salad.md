# 🍕 Naan Pepperoni Pizzas with Strawberry Goat Cheese Salad

**Serves 6**

## 🍕 Pizza

### Ingredients

- 6 naan breads
- 1½ cups marinara or pizza sauce
- 3 cups shredded mozzarella
- 30–40 pepperoni slices

### Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
2. Spread sauce on naan.
3. Add cheese and pepperoni.
4. Bake 8–10 minutes until cheese is melted.

## 🥗 Salad

### Ingredients

- 10–12 oz spring mix
- 1 pint strawberries, sliced
- 1 small red onion, thinly sliced
- 4 oz goat cheese
- ¾ cup candied pecans
- 2 tbsp honey or balsamic glaze
Toss greens and strawberries. Add toppings and drizzle.
