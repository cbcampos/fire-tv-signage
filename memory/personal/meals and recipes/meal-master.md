🍽️ Meal 1: Super Green Pasta + Strawberry Goat Cheese Salad

Serves 6

⸻

🥬 Super Green Pasta

Ingredients
	•	12 oz spiral pasta
	•	3 tbsp olive oil
	•	5 cloves garlic, sliced
	•	1 large shallot, sliced
	•	2 medium zucchini, cubed
	•	6 packed cups fresh spinach
	•	1½ cups fresh basil leaves
	•	1¼ tsp salt
	•	1 cup reserved pasta water
	•	⅓ cup heavy cream
	•	¾ cup grated Parmesan, plus more for serving
	•	Zest of 1 lemon

Instructions
	1.	Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook pasta until al dente. Reserve 1 cup pasta water, then drain.
	2.	Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add garlic and shallot. Cook 2–3 minutes until fragrant.
	3.	Add zucchini and cook 4–5 minutes until softened.
	4.	Add spinach and cook until wilted.
	5.	Transfer vegetable mixture to a blender. Add basil, cream, Parmesan, salt, and ½–1 cup pasta water. Blend until smooth.
	6.	Toss sauce with pasta. Add lemon zest and extra Parmesan before serving.

⸻

🍓 Strawberry Goat Cheese Salad

Ingredients
	•	6 cups mixed greens
	•	2–3 cups sliced strawberries
	•	4 oz goat cheese, crumbled
	•	2 tbsp olive oil
	•	Balsamic glaze (optional)

Instructions
	1.	Toss greens and strawberries.
	2.	Top with goat cheese.
	3.	Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic glaze before serving.

🍗 Meal 2: Creamy Chicken Orzo

Serves 6

Ingredients
	•	3 large boneless skinless chicken breasts (about 2½ lb)
	•	Salt, black pepper, sweet paprika
	•	1½ tbsp olive oil
	•	1 tbsp butter
	•	1 medium onion, diced
	•	3 cloves garlic, minced
	•	13 oz orzo (about 2 cups)
	•	3 cups chicken broth
	•	1½ cups water
	•	¾ cup heavy cream
	•	6 oz cream cheese
	•	½ cup grated Parmesan
	•	½ cup chopped parsley

Instructions
	1.	Season chicken with salt, pepper, and paprika.
	2.	Heat olive oil in a large skillet. Sear chicken 3–4 minutes per side until cooked through. Remove and slice.
	3.	In the same pan, melt butter. Sauté onion until soft, then add garlic.
	4.	Stir in orzo and toast 1 minute.
	5.	Add broth and water. Simmer 10–12 minutes until orzo is tender.
	6.	Stir in cream cheese, heavy cream, Parmesan, parsley, salt, and pepper.
	7.	Return sliced chicken to pan and serve warm.

🥣 Meal 3: Creamy Kale, White Bean & Sausage Soup

Serves 6

Ingredients
	•	1½ lb smoked sausage, sliced
	•	1½ tbsp olive oil
	•	1 large onion, diced
	•	4 cloves garlic, minced
	•	6 cups chicken broth
	•	2 (15-oz) cans white beans, drained
	•	3 medium Yukon gold potatoes, cubed
	•	1 tsp smoked paprika
	•	½ tsp black pepper
	•	3 cups chopped kale
	•	¾ cup heavy cream
	•	Salt to taste
	•	French bread for serving

Instructions
	1.	Brown sausage in olive oil. Remove and set aside.
	2.	In same pot, sauté onion and garlic.
	3.	Add broth, beans, potatoes, paprika, and pepper. Simmer 15 minutes.
	4.	Add kale and sausage. Simmer 3–4 minutes.
	5.	Stir in cream and adjust salt. Serve with bread.

## 🥶 Meal 50: Classic Gazpacho with Grilled Ham & Cheese Sandwiches

Serves 6

⸻

🍅 Gazpacho

Ingredients
- 3 lbs ripe tomatoes (about 6 medium) — Roma are classic
- 1 English cucumber, peeled and roughly chopped (reserve ¼ cup finely diced for garnish)
- 1 red bell pepper, roughly chopped (seeds removed)
- ½ small red onion, roughly chopped (rinse under cold water to tame sharpness)
- 2 cloves garlic, smashed
- 1 small Fresno chile or jalapeño, seeds removed (optional — adds gentle heat)
- ⅓ cup extra-virgin olive oil
- 3 tbsp red wine vinegar (or sherry vinegar)
- 1 tsp honey (optional — rounds out acidic tomatoes)
- 1 tsp salt, plus more to taste
- ½ tsp black pepper
- ¼ cup fresh cilantro (optional)

**For garnish:**
- Reserved diced cucumber
- Diced tomato
- Drizzle of olive oil
- Small basil leaves or extra cilantro

⸻

🥪 Grilled Ham & Cheese

Ingredients
- 12 slices good sandwich bread (sourdough, Texas toast, or country white)
- 12 slices deli ham (honey ham, black forest, or smoked)
- 12 slices Swiss, Gruyère, or sharp cheddar cheese
- 3 tbsp softened butter (or mayo)
- Optional: thin slices of tomato, pickle, or Dijon mustard

⸻

**Instructions (Gazpacho):**
1. Core and roughly chop tomatoes. If using very large or watery tomatoes, halve and squeeze out the seeds over the sink (less watery soup).
2. Add tomatoes, cucumber, bell pepper, onion, garlic, chile, olive oil, vinegar, honey, salt, pepper, and cilantro to a blender. Puree on high 60–90 seconds until smooth and vibrant red.
3. Taste and adjust — more salt, vinegar, or olive oil as needed. Soup should be tangy, savory, and balanced.
4. (Optional) Strain through fine-mesh for silkier texture. Skip for rustic.
5. Transfer to sealed container. **Refrigerate at least 2 hours** (overnight is even better).
6. Serve in bowls with reserved diced cucumber, diced tomato, drizzle of olive oil, and herb leaves.

**Instructions (Grilled Ham & Cheese):**
1. Butter one side of each bread slice (or use mayo for better browning).
2. Build sandwiches: bread (butter side down), cheese, ham, optional tomato/pickle, cheese, bread (butter side up). Double cheese keeps the filling glued.
3. Heat skillet over medium-low. Cook 3–4 minutes per side until bread is deep golden and cheese is fully melted. Cover the pan for the second side to help cheese melt evenly.
4. Slice diagonally and serve alongside gazpacho.

⸻

**Notes:**
- Gazpacho MUST chill 2+ hours. Make it in the morning (or night before) so it's ready by dinner.
- Soup keeps 3 days refrigerated — flavor deepens daily.
- For kid-friendly gazpacho: skip the chile.
- Grilled cheese on sourdough with sharp cheddar + ham is the classic combo. Gruyère melts smoother if you want fancier.
- Don't freeze gazpacho — texture breaks when thawed.

## 🍝 Meal 54: Creamy Tomato Gnocchi with Italian Sausage & Spinach

Serves 6

**Added 2026-07-10 — first run 2026-07-09, liked by family. One-pan, ~30 min, no pasta boiling required (gnocchi cooks right in the sauce).**

⸻

🍝 Main

**Ingredients:**
- 1½ lbs Italian sausage (mild or hot), casings removed
- 1 (16 oz) package shelf-stable or refrigerated gnocchi
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 small onion, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 (28 oz) can crushed tomatoes
- 1 cup heavy cream (or half-and-half)
- ½ tsp dried basil
- ½ tsp dried oregano
- ½ tsp red pepper flakes (optional)
- 5 oz fresh baby spinach
- ½ cup grated Parmesan
- Salt and pepper to taste
