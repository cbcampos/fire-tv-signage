## Tue 4/28 — Black Bean Burritos with Spanish Rice

Serves 6

🌯 Black Bean Burritos

Ingredients:
- 6 (10 inch) flour tortillas
- 6 tbsp vegetable oil
- 3 small onions, chopped
- 2 red bell peppers, chopped
- 1 tbsp minced garlic
- 1 tbsp minced jalapeño peppers
- 3 (15 oz) cans black beans, rinsed and drained
- 9 oz cream cheese, cubed
- 1½ tsp salt
- 6 tbsp chopped fresh cilantro
- 1½ cups shredded cheese (cheddar or mexican blend)
- Sour cream, for serving
- Salsa, for serving

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Wrap tortillas in foil and bake 15 minutes until heated through.
2. Meanwhile, heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion, bell pepper, garlic, and jalapeño; cook and stir for 2 minutes.
3. Stir in beans and cook until heated through, about 3 minutes. Stir in cream cheese and salt; cook, stirring occasionally, for 2 minutes. Stir in cilantro.
4. Spoon filling across the middle of each tortilla. Top with shredded cheese. Fold opposing edges to overlap filling, then roll into a burrito.
5. Serve immediately with sour cream and salsa.

🍚 Spanish Rice (Side)

Ingredients:
- 2 boxes Spanish rice mix (Vigo or Zatarain's)
- Each box: 2 cups water + 1 tbsp butter

Instructions:
1. Cook Spanish rice per package instructions.
2. Serve alongside burritos.

## Thu 4/30 — Super Green Pasta + Strawberry Goat Cheese Salad

Serves 6

🥬 Super Green Pasta

Ingredients:
- 12 oz spiral pasta
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 5 cloves garlic, sliced
- 1 large shallot, sliced
- 2 medium zucchini, cubed
- 6 packed cups fresh spinach
- 1½ cups fresh basil leaves
- 1¼ tsp salt
- 1 cup reserved pasta water
- ⅓ cup heavy cream
- ¾ cup grated Parmesan, plus more for serving
- Zest of 1 lemon

Instructions:
1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook pasta until al dente. Reserve 1 cup pasta water, then drain.
2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add garlic and shallot. Cook 2–3 minutes until fragrant.
3. Add zucchini and cook 4–5 minutes until softened.
4. Add spinach and cook until wilted.
5. Transfer vegetable mixture to a blender. Add basil, cream, Parmesan, salt, and ½–1 cup pasta water. Blend until smooth.
6. Toss sauce with pasta. Add lemon zest and extra Parmesan before serving.

🍓 Strawberry Goat Cheese Salad

Ingredients:
- 6 cups mixed greens
- 2–3 cups sliced strawberries
- 4 oz goat cheese, crumbled
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Balsamic glaze (optional)

Instructions:
1. Toss greens and strawberries.
2. Top with goat cheese.
3. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic glaze before serving.

## Sun 5/3 — French Toast Casserole and Sausage

Serves 8

🥞 French Toast Casserole

Ingredients:
- ½ loaf French bread, cubed (about 8 oz)
- ½ cup butter
- 1 cup packed brown sugar
- 6 eggs
- 2 cups milk
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 2 teaspoons cinnamon, divided
- 1 tablespoon brown sugar (topping)
- Powdered sugar (optional topping)

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Butter a 13x9 inch baking pan.
2. Melt butter in saucepan over medium heat. Stir in brown sugar until dissolved and smooth. Pour into bottom of 9x13 pan and spread evenly.
3. Add bread cubes on top of brown sugar/butter mixture.
4. Whisk together eggs, milk, vanilla, and 1 tsp cinnamon in a medium bowl. Pour over bread, ensuring all pieces are saturated. Sprinkle with remaining 1 tsp cinnamon and 1 tbsp brown sugar.
5. Bake 25–30 minutes until golden brown. Top with powdered sugar if desired.

🍖 Sausage:
- 1½ lb smoked sausage, sliced and browned in a skillet

## Mon 5/4 — Dirty Rice with Sautéed Zucchini & Peppers

Serves 6

🍚 Dirty Rice

Ingredients:
- 1 lb ground turkey
- 2 cups long-grain white rice, cooked
- 1 onion, diced
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 2 celery stalks, diced
- 1 green bell pepper, diced
- 2 teaspoons Cajun seasoning
- ½ teaspoon smoked paprika
- Salt and pepper to taste
- ½ cup chicken broth

🥒 Sautéed Zucchini & Peppers:
- 2 medium zucchini, diced
- 1 red bell pepper, diced
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- Salt, pepper, garlic powder

Instructions:
1. Brown ground turkey in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Drain any excess fat.
2. Add onion, celery, and green pepper. Sauté 4–5 minutes until softened.
3. Add garlic, Cajun seasoning, paprika. Cook 1 minute until fragrant.
4. Add cooked rice, chicken broth. Stir to combine and simmer 5 minutes.
5. In a separate pan, sauté zucchini and red pepper in olive oil until tender and slightly caramelized.
6. Serve dirty rice topped with sautéed vegetables.
