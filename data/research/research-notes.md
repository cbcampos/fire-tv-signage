
## VibeVoice (Microsoft) — saved 2026-04-28
https://github.com/microsoft/VibeVoice

Open-source frontier voice AI family:
- **VibeVoice-ASR-7B** ✅ Live — 60-min single-pass speech recognition + speaker diarization + timestamps. 50+ languages. Natively in HF Transformers. [HF model](https://huggingface.co/microsoft/VibeVoice-ASR) | [Playground](https://aka.ms/vibevoice-asr)
- **VibeVoice-Realtime-0.5B** ✅ Live — Streaming TTS, 9 languages, 11 English style voices. [HF model](https://huggingface.co/microsoft/VibeVoice-Realtime-0.5B)
- **VibeVoice-TTS-1.5B** ❌ Removed Sept 2025 — was 90-min multi-speaker TTS, pulled after misuse discovered.

Use cases to explore: long-form voice note transcription, meeting diarization, potential local TTS alternative (Realtime-0.5B on Mac).
